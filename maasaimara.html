Maasai Mara

Legendary savannahs, the Great Migration, big cats, hot air balloon safaris & rich Maasai culture. Dive into the activities below.

Top Activities in the Mara

Each activity card includes a short silent preview video plus an accessible description. Expand Details to read more.

Guided Game Drives & Big Cats

Early morning & late afternoon drives give you the best chance to see lions, cheetahs, leopards, elephants, and more in golden light.

Details / Transcript

This clip shows a pride of lions lounging after a hunt. Guides maintain safe distances to protect wildlife and visitors.

Best time: Dawn & dusk game drives. Dry season (Jul–Oct) increases sightings as grass is shorter.

Great Migration (River Crossing)

Watch the beautiful scenes of the river flow its so beautiful.

Details / Transcript

Shot at the Mara River during peak migration. Guides position vehicles at safe, non-disruptive distances. Crossings are unpredictable – patience is key!

Peak months: Jul-Sep (sometimes into Oct).

Hot Air Balloon Safari

Float silently over the plains at sunrise, then land to a champagne bush breakfast. Epic aerial views of wildlife & rolling savannah.

Details / Transcript

Clip shows sunrise lift-off, sweeping landscape shots, and wildlife herds from above. Accessible to most travelers; weight limits apply.

Wear layers - it's cold pre-sunrise. Book early in peak months.

Sundowner Bush Drinks & Dinner

Wrap your safari day with sunset drinks on the open plains, followed by a bush dinner under the stars. Unreal vibes.

Details / Transcript

Expect portable chairs, drinks, and a safe open area chosen by your guides. After sunset, lighting helps guests with low vision move safely.

Notify camp of dietary or mobility needs ahead of time – most lodges can accommodate.
