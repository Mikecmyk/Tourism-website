Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Sunset Drive

Land in Nairobi, transfer to Maasai Mara, settle at the lodge, and enjoy a relaxing sunset game drive.

Day 2: Full-Day Safari

Explore the reserve on a full-day game drive. Spot lions, elephants, giraffes, and more with picnic lunch included.

Day 3: Maasai Village Visit

Immerse in culture by visiting a traditional Maasai village. Learn customs, dance, and take part in storytelling.

Day 4: Hot Air Balloon Safari

Rise early for a breathtaking sunrise balloon safari followed by champagne breakfast in the wild.

Day 5: Departure

Pack up, relax with a light breakfast, and say goodbye to the savannah as you head back to the city.
