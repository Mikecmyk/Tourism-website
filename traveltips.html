Travel Tips

Your ultimate guide to smooth adventures

Best Times to Visit

The best time to visit Kenya is from July to October during the Great Migration and dry season, making safaris and outdoor adventures perfect.

Weather Overview

Dry Season

June - October
Sunny, low rainfall, great for safaris.

Short Rains

November - December
Light rains, still good for travel.

Long Rains

March - May
Wet, less ideal for outdoor activities.

Essential Travel Tips

1. Pack Smart

Carry only the essentials and use packing cubes to save space. Don't forget your power bank and travel adapter.

2. Stay Safe

Keep copies of important documents and avoid displaying expensive gadgets in public places.

3. Learn Local Basics

Learn a few local phrases and cultural tips to easily connect with locals and avoid misunderstandings.

4. Book in Advance

Book hotels, flights, and activities early to get better deals and avoid last-minute rush.
